World's Top 5 Dishes

Sushi - A traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, raw fish, and vegetables.

Burger - A classic American dish made with a ground beef patty, lettuce, tomato, and various condiments.

Tacos - A traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling.

Pad Thai - A stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food and at casual local eateries in Thailand.
