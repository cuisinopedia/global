New Features

Interactive Recipe Filters

Filter recipes based on dietary preferences, cuisine type, cooking time, and difficulty level.

Example Implementation: Use dropdown menus or checkboxes with JavaScript to dynamically display recipes based on user selections.

Meal Planner Tool

Plan your weekly meals, select recipes for each day, generate a shopping list, and get nutritional information.

Example Implementation: Drag-and-drop interface with integration to APIs like Spoonacular for nutritional data.

AI-Powered Recipe Recommendations

Receive personalized recipe suggestions based on your preferences, available ingredients, and seasonal produce.

Example Implementation: Integrate AI APIs (such as OpenAI or Spoonacular) and a "What’s in Your Fridge?" feature.

Augmented Reality (AR) Plating Guide

Use your phone camera to get overlayed plating suggestions, helping you plate like a professional chef.

Example Implementation: Leverage AR libraries like AR.js or platforms like Google ARCore.

Voice-Activated Cooking Assistant

Have recipe steps read aloud, set timers, and convert measurements hands-free.

Example Implementation: Utilize the Web Speech API and integrate with assistants like Alexa or Google Assistant.

Seasonal and Trending Recipes

Discover recipes that are in season or trending based on searches and social media.

Example Implementation: Fetch trending recipes via APIs (e.g., Spoonacular) and create a dedicated section.

Food Pairing Guide

Get recommendations on pairing your dishes with wines, beers, side dishes, or desserts.

Example Implementation: Integrate food pairing APIs to build a visual guide with images and descriptions.

Multilingual Support

Access recipes in multiple languages and easily switch between them.

Example Implementation: Use translation APIs (like Google Translate) and store translations in JSON files.

Food Blog

Read articles on food trends, chef interviews, and tips for hosting memorable events.

Example Implementation: Integrate a CMS or a commenting system like Disqus.

Sustainability Tips

Learn how to reduce food waste and adopt eco-friendly kitchen practices.

Example Implementation: Create a dedicated section with infographics and videos on sustainability.

Virtual Food Tours

Take interactive, 360° tours of famous food markets, restaurants, and street food stalls around the world.

Example Implementation: Use 360-degree videos or Google Street View with clickable hotspots.
